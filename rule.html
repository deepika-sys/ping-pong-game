Rules

For one player game: ⚽

Player can move paddle left ⬅ and right ➡ on board and try to hit the ball with paddlle as many as can. Game will be over when player miss three hits.

For two player game: ⚽

Player can move paddle up ⬆ and down ⬇ on board and try to hit the ball with paddlle as many as can. Game will be over when either player 1 or 2 miss three hits.
